Spicy Cheddar and Paprika cookies



Dough: 01 Click here the base dough recipe.

  • all purpose flour: 200g
  • butter: 150g
  • salt: to taste, but at least half a teaspoon
  • ice cold water: 30g
  • Cheddar cheese: to taste
  • Ground paprika powder: to taste
  • Cayenne pepper: to taste

For lactose intolerant people: you can replace butter 1:1 with any sort of solid fat except coconut fat.

add flour, butter, ice cold water, salt, cayenne pepper, paprika powder to the bowl,
cut up the butter beforehand and work it into a doughball. the ball of dough should
be relatively soft and pliable, but hold its shape and not smear. add the cheese
and work it into a uniform dough ball. refrigerate for one hour. make into
cookies and bake for 7 minutes at 200c.


baking time is not absolute, it may take faster or slower for you,
but the cookies should be very slightly darker at the edges to know
when they are done visually.

the dough makes for airy cookies, some may form bubbles in it.
the cookies do not spread while baking, they will shrink slightly when cooling.
